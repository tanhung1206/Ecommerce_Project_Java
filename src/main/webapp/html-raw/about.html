Our Story

Revolutionizing online shopping since 2010

Who We Are

ShopEase was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to make online shopping easy, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone. What started as a small startup with just 5 employees has grown into a global e-commerce platform serving millions of customers worldwide.

Our commitment to quality products, competitive prices, and exceptional customer service has made us a trusted name in the e-commerce industry. We believe in creating a seamless shopping experience that puts our customers first.

Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

ShopEase was founded in a small apartment with just 5 team members and a vision to revolutionize online shopping.

2013

First Milestone

Reached 100,000 customers and expanded our product catalog to include over 5,000 items across multiple categories.

2015

International Expansion

Expanded operations to 25 countries and launched our mobile app, making shopping on-the-go easier than ever.

2018

Innovation Award

Received the E-commerce Innovation Award for our personalized shopping experience and customer service excellence.

2020

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our sustainability initiative, committing to eco-friendly packaging and carbon-neutral shipping options.

2023

Today

Serving over 10 million customers worldwide with a team of 500+ dedicated employees across 15 offices globally.
